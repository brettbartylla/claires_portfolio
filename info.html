Claire Loes is a designer and cellist currently based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This past December they earned their BFA in graphic design from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design.

Their work embraces an intersection of handmade and digital image–making, and are currently inspired by the ascii art of Alan Riddell, the music of Lena Platonos, and (forever and always) the collaborative work of Nam June Paik and Charlotte Moorman. Claire is currently biking around South Minneapolis, baking, and knitting sweaters in their spare time, and really misses going dancing.
